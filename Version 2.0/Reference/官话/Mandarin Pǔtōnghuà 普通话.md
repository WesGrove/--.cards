## Survival Phrases
- Hello: 你好 (nǐ hǎo)  
- Thank you: 谢谢 (xièxie)  
- Yes / No: 是 (shì) / 不是 (bù shì)  
- How much?: 多少钱？(duōshao qián?)  

## Tips
1. Start with Pinyin and tones.  
2. Learn 100 most-used characters first.  
3. Listen daily—tones take practice.
