汉字 (Hànzì)

1. What Exactly Are Hanzi?

Each Hanzi represents one syllable and one meaning.
Think of them as building blocks: string them together to form words, phrases, and sentences.

2. Two Scripts in Use Today

Script Mainly Used In Notes
Simplified Mainland China Fewer strokes, easier to write
Traditional Taiwan, Hong Kong Historic form, more strokes

3. How Many Do You Need?

Level Hanzi Needed What You Can Read
Daily life ~3,500 99% of modern texts
Well-educated reader ~8,000+ Novels, newspapers

4. Universal Stroke-Order Rules

Order Rule
1st Top → bottom
2nd Left → right
3rd Outside → inside

5. Daily Study Recipe

  • Goal: 10–20 new Hanzi per day
  • Tool: any spaced-repetition app (Anki, Skritter, Pleco)
  • Review cycle: 24 h → 72 h → 7 d → 30 d
